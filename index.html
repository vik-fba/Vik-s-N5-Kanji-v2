Where is my data saved? Your progress (Got it / Still learning cards, SRS intervals, custom decks) is saved in your browser's localStorage — storage built into every browser tied to the URL you're visiting. It persists when you close the tab but will be lost if you clear your browser history or cache. Data is not shared between devices or browsers. If you sign in with an account, a per-account copy is also saved locally under your email key, but it is still local to that browser — not synced to a server.

Privacy: No data is sent to any server. Everything stays on your device.
